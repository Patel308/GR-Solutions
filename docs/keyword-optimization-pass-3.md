## Safety and cannibalization decisions

- Service-center phrases are answered with explicit independent-repair wording. No official or authorized brand affiliation is claimed.
- Near-me and shop-style terms consolidate into `/services` or `/services/tv-repair-near-me`; no doorway pages were created.
- Six related cost terms consolidate into the existing Delhi screen-replacement guide; no thin cost pages were created.
- `UrbanClap`/`Urban Company`, Gaffar Market and unverified “best” terms were excluded because they imply competitor affiliation, unverified location or superiority. The selection continued down the cluster list until 50 safe terms were included.
- The source keyword CSV and research files were not modified.
