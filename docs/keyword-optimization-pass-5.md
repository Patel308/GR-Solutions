## SEO safety decisions

- No new page was created. Every keyword maps to an established city, service-city or cost-guide canonical.
- Neighborhood names remain supporting local context; they do not get standalone pages.
- OLED and QLED cost phrases share one Delhi NCR decision guide because the pricing factors overlap and fixed-price pages would be misleading.
- QLED TV repair Noida remains on the existing combined OLED/QLED Noida page.
- No fake prices, ratings, branch addresses, official service-center claims or “best” claims were added.
- The supplied keyword CSV and research report were not modified.
